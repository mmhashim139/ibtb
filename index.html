About this website

We tell the story of Ireland and her people through its music, art, literature and the unique culture and heritage of the Irish people. From passage tombs older than the pyramids to cities founded by Vikings, crumbling castles to grand country estates, Ireland's history is etched its landscape. Explore the spectacular heartlands, coastal cities, the wild Atlantic way and much more unique activities!
